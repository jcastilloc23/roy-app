Pricing FAQs

Questions about pricing

Can I try Pro for free?
Yes — every paid plan comes with a 14-day free trial, no credit card required. You'll get full access to all Pro features and can downgrade to Free at any time.
What happens if I go over my source limit on the Free plan?
You'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro. Your existing connections and data are always preserved — you'll never lose your history.
Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account settings. If you downgrade mid-cycle, you'll retain Pro access until the end of the billing period.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing?
Yes — annual billing saves you 2 months compared to paying monthly. Pro annually is $99/year (save $45). Team annually is $390/year (save $78).
